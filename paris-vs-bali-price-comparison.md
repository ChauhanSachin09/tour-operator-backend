## Verdict & Recommendations

### Best for Budget Travelers → **Bali**
Land costs are 50–60% lower than Paris. A 7-day Bali trip can be done comfortably for **$350–$420 (land only)** vs Paris at **$784+**. Even factoring in flights, Bali wins on overall affordability.

### Best Flight + Hotel Deal → **Paris** (via KAYAK / JetBlue Vacations)
KAYAK bundles start at just **$129** and JetBlue Vacations at **$896** (4 nights, round-trip), undercutting Bali's cheapest bundle at **$1,505** — though Paris packages are typically shorter stays.

### Best Value for Inclusions → **Bali** (BaliCheapestTours / TourRadar)
At **$50–$60/day**, Bali packages include 4-star accommodation, breakfast, transfers, guided excursions, and entrance fees — extraordinary value compared to equivalent Paris offerings at 2–3× the price.

### Best for Short City Breaks → **Paris** (Viator / Trip.com)
Both cities start day tours at **$14/person**, but Paris offers more structured, world-class cultural experiences (Louvre, Versailles, Eiffel Tower) packaged into single-day guided tours.
